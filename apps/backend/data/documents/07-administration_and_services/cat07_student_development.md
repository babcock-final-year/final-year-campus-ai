## Vice President for Student Development — Role

The VPSD:
- Has overall responsibility for student welfare, conduct, and development
- Approves off-campus living concessions and vehicle registration
- Signs off on all leave/exeat requests above the Hall Administrator's authority
- Oversees the disciplinary pipeline (Deans Council → SDC → ADCOM)
- Co-signs graduation clearance for non-suspended students
- Approves the SSC's special needs recommendations
- Sets and communicates curfew adjustments in special circumstances

## Campus Life Committee (CLC)

The **Campus Life Committee (CLC)** approves all student social and extracurricular activities. Requests must be submitted through the **Hall Administrator** or **Student Activities Office** at least **2 weeks in advance**.

CLC oversees:
- Hall social programmes (Open Day, banquets, drama)
- Off-campus activity requests
- Get-togethers and parties
- Academic field trips and club trips
- Sports events and inter-faculty competitions
