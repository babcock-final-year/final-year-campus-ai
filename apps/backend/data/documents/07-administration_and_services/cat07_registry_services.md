## Overview

The **Office of the Registrar** provides academic and administrative services to students, faculty, staff, alumni, and parents. It is the official custodian of all academic records, manages admissions and enrollment, oversees course registration, and certifies graduation requirements.

## Graduation Clearance Process

Final clearance for graduating students requires sign-off from all of the following:

1. Head of Department (HOD)
2. School Dean
3. Library
4. Bursary (fees clearance)
5. Health Centre
6. Accommodation Office
7. VP Student Development
8. Director of Security Services
9. Registry

> The **Bursary Clearance Form** and **Final Clearance Form** must both be completed. Certificate collection follows the official procedure available at babcock.edu.ng/student-forms.

## Certificate Collection

For collection of certificates by a proxy (another person on behalf of the graduate):
1. Signed letter of authority from the graduate, addressed to the Registrar
2. ID card of the graduate
3. ID card of the person collecting

## Course Registration & Add/Drop

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Add/drop period | Within the first few weeks of semester — see academic calendar |
| Minimum credit load | 16 credit units per semester |
| Extra unit form | Required to exceed standard credit load |
| Course Adviser signature | Required on all course registration forms |

---

## Change of Programme

- Submit the **Change of Course Form** from babcock.edu.ng/student-forms
- Requires: HOD (current and proposed) → Dean (current and proposed) → Registrar → SVP Academic
- Subject to available space and meeting entry requirements for the new programme
- Not permitted after the add/drop deadline
