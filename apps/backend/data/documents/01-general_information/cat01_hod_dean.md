## How to Reach an HOD or Dean

If you don't know your HOD's contact, you can:
1. Visit your **School Secretary's office** in your school building
2. Email the **Registry**: registry@babcock.edu.ng
3. Check the official university website: https://babcock.edu.ng
4. Ask your **Course Adviser** — each department assigns course advisers to students per level

---

## Course Advisers

Every student is assigned a **Course Adviser** within their department. Course Advisers:
- Guide course registration each semester
- Sign course registration forms
- Provide academic counselling and progression advice
- Are the first point of contact for academic issues before escalating to the HOD

> Course Adviser assignments are communicated at the start of each semester. Check your departmental notice board or student portal.
