## Overview

Spiritual development is **one of the primary missions** of Babcock University. As a Seventh-day Adventist institution, BU integrates spiritual life into the fabric of its academic calendar. All students — regardless of religious background — are expected to respect and support the university's religious programmes.

## Annual Spiritual Events

### Youth Week of Prayer

- Held **once per session** in the First Semester
- Campus-wide spiritual emphasis for students
- Special guest speaker leads daily services across worship centres
- 2025/2026: **Saturday, October 18 – Saturday, October 25, 2025**
- No cancellation of classes required, but worship attendance is encouraged

---

### Week of Spiritual Emphasis (WSE)

- The most significant spiritual event of the academic year
- Held during **Second Semester** each session
- **All academic lectures are paused** during this week
- A distinguished guest speaker (pastor, evangelist, or evangelist team) is invited
- Multiple daily services: morning, midday, and evening
- Held across the main worship centres campus-wide
- 2025/2026: **Saturday, February 7 – Saturday, February 14, 2026**

---

### Nigeria Association of Adventist Students (NAAS) Week

- Annual campus week for the SDA student association
- Spiritually, academically, and socially focused
- Open to all students
- 2025/2026: **Sunday, February 22 – Saturday, February 28, 2026**

---

### Founder's Day Thanksgiving

- Part of the **Founder's Day** celebrations on September 17
- Includes a **Thanksgiving Sabbath Worship Service**
- Special recognition of long-serving faculty and staff retirees
- Combined celebration of university founding and staff faithfulness

---

### Baccalaureate Service

- Held on the **Sabbath (Saturday)** of Graduation Week
- A solemn worship service for graduating students and their families
- Gives spiritual commission to graduates before Convocation
- 2026 date: **Saturday, July 25, 2026** at 8:30 AM

---

### Consecration Service

- Held the **Friday evening** before the Convocation Ceremony (Sunday)
- Venue: **Amphitheater**
- Spiritually prepares graduating students for the formal Convocation the next day
- 2026 date: **Friday, July 24, 2026** at 6:30 PM

---

### Graduation Sabbath Thanksgiving / Vesper Service

- Held on the **Sabbath of Graduation Week**
- Thanksgiving worship and closing vesper before the final Convocation day
- Venue: Amphitheater
- 2025 (Charis): Saturday, July 26, 2025

## Sabbath Rules on Campus

- Friday is treated as **preparation day** for the Sabbath (sunset Friday to sunset Saturday)
- No inter-hall visitations on Fridays (hall sanitation and Sabbath preparation)
- No social events or entertainment permitted on Sabbath (Friday sunset to Saturday sunset)
- Cooking and non-essential activities are restricted
- Students who violate Sabbath observance rules face **30 demerit points**
- All room clean-up and inspection must be completed before Friday evening
