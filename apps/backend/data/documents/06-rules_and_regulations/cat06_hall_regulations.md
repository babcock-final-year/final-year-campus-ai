# Babcock University — Hall of Residence Regulations

**Category:** Rules & Regulations
**Subcategory:** Residence Hall Rules
**Source:** BU Student Handbook 2021–2024; Final Year Bot Training Document
**Last Updated:** 8th March, 2026
**Format:** Markdown

---

## Overview

Each hall of residence has its own internal regulations **in addition to** those in the Student Handbook. It is the responsibility of every hall resident to know and abide by both sets of regulations.

---

## Check-In Rules

- All financially registered students must check in at their hall **not earlier than 12:00 noon** on the official day of resumption
- Check-in must be done formally through the Hall Administrator using the **Check-In Form**
- Checking in without hall administration knowledge: **₦1,000/day fine + immediate eviction**
- Failing to check in after online registration: **₦1,000/day fine**

---

## Daily Schedule & Room Conduct

| Time | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| 5:30 am – 5:45 am | Morning devotion in individual rooms |
| 5:45 am | Earliest time to leave the residence hall |
| 9:45 pm | All students must be in their residence halls |
| 11:00 pm | **Lights-out** — mandatory for all students |
| 10:00 pm – 5:45 am | Curfew — no student may be outside halls without permission |

- After 11:00 pm, **only desk lamps** are permitted (provided by students); lamp rays must not extend beyond the student's reading area
- Absolute silence must be maintained after 11:00 pm
- All students must be in their own rooms or in the lounge/reading room by lights-out

---

## Room Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Room access | No student may enter another student's room or tamper with their belongings without express permission; violation creates replacement liability for any reported theft or damage |
| Room keys | No student shall have access to another student's room key; violation creates replacement liability; lost key cost is borne by the student |
| Room condition | Rooms must be in proper order at all times; a room left in unsatisfactory condition at end of semester will be restored to standard and occupants surcharged |
| Walls | Nails, posters, tacks, paint, or anything that may damage walls are prohibited |
| Furniture | Moving furniture or property between rooms is not allowed; no alterations to furniture, fixtures, lights, woodwork, or locks without the Hall Administrator's prior consent |
| Mattresses | Bed-sharing and transfer of mattresses between rooms/students is prohibited; mattresses must be on proper bed frames, not on bare floors |
| End of semester | All rooms must be vacated by **12:00 noon** the day after the last exam; all personal effects must be removed |
| Garbage | Dropping or throwing garbage in rooms or corridors is not tolerated |

---

## Friday Room Inspection

Every Friday, a mandatory **room inspection** and **general clean-up** is held:

| Time | Activity |
|------|---------|
| 3:00 pm – 4:00 pm | Room clean-up (all students must participate) |
| 4:00 pm – 5:00 pm | Room inspection by Hall Administrator / RHA |

- All students must be in their rooms from 3:00 pm – 5:00 pm on Fridays
- All sporting and student activities must end **before 3:00 pm on Fridays**
- The Hall Administrator must be notified if a student cannot participate due to a valid reason
- This exercise runs every Friday including the last Friday of the semester before examination

## Visitation Rules

| Scenario | Permitted Hours / Rules |
|----------|------------------------|
| Opposite-sex visitors (reception only) | Sunday – Thursday, 8:00 am – 6:00 pm; **no visitation on Fridays** (hall sanitation + Sabbath prep) or on **Sabbath** (worship and rest) |
| Same-sex inter-hall visitation | Lounge only; not beyond 9:45 pm |
| Intra-hall visitation | Allowed; not beyond 9:45 pm |
| Off-campus students visiting halls | Reception only; must sign in at the porter's lodge |
| Visitors from outside after hours | Requires written approval from Hall Administrator |
| Overnight visitors | **Absolutely prohibited**; all room members are jointly and severally liable |

## Electrical Appliances

- Cooking appliances of **any kind** are prohibited in rooms: **60 demerit points**
- Prohibited appliances include electric cookers, hotplates, immersion heaters, and similar items
- Only desk lamps (student-provided) are approved for use in rooms after lights-out
- Unauthorised use of university property or appliances: **30 demerit points**

---

## Personal Property & Security

- Students are discouraged from bringing phones worth more than **₦50,000** (university-set limit; confirm current amount with VPSD)
- In the event of theft, restitution is capped at this amount regardless of actual value
- All lost-and-found items are handled by the Security Department

---

## Room Assignment & Changes

- Room assignments are done through the accommodation office
- Changing rooms without authorisation is not permitted
- Whenever a student first enters a hall, changes rooms, or withdraws, the room is inspected and documented
- Hall Residence Assistants (RHAs) conduct routine room checks as arranged by the Hall Administrator
- The Hall Administrator has authority to inspect rooms **at any time**

---

## Check-Out Rules

- Check-out must be done through the Hall Administrator using the official **Check-Out Form**
- Improper check-out with key: **₦2,000 + lock replacement + door replacement if damaged**
- Before leaving campus for any reason, students must check out in accordance with residence hall regulations
