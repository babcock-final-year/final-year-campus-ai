## Overview

The **Laz Otti Memorial Library** is the central academic library of Babcock University, situated at the heart of the core academic area on the Ilishan-Remo campus. It was commissioned in **May 2014** and named after the father of donor Dr. Alex Otti, a former member of the University's Governing Council.

The library has evolved from its origins as a college library in 1959 through a seminary library phase and into its current university library status.

**Vision:** A top-rank provider of research and instructional materials in a user-friendly environment encapsulated with missionary zeal and infectious competence.

**Mission:** Support the university mission through the provision of current and broad-spectrum research, teaching, and learning materials for transforming lives and impacting society positively.

## Internet & Digital Access

- Internet access is **free** for all registered students and staff
- No password required to connect to the internet within library premises
- Outside the library, **passwords for subscribed e-journals** are issued to registered users on request for remote access
- Several computers connected to the library server, managed by the Systems Librarian

## Branch Libraries

There are **5 branch libraries** located within their respective schools/departments:

| Branch | Location | Serves |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **EAH 1 Library** | Right wing, upstairs — School of Education & Humanities | Religion and Theology students |
| **EAH 2 Library** | Downstairs below EAH 1 | History and International Studies students |
| **SAT 1 Library** | School of Science & Technology | Science, Medical, and Allied Health courses (except Nursing) |
| **SAT 2 Library** | Directly below SAT 1 | Nursing students |
| **BUCC Library**  | New NH Building | Project materials and resources |
| **Law Library** | Iperu Campus | Law students — fully air-conditioned, internet-driven; stocks Law Reports, Law Reviews, Supreme Court Judgments, high-profile Journals |

## Library Regulations

- Food and drinks are not permitted in the library
- Mobile phones must be on silent mode
- Bags must be deposited at the bag deposit area before entry
- The library is a quiet study zone — boisterous behaviour carries **40 demerit points**
- The library maintains a reference section; reference materials are for in-library use only
