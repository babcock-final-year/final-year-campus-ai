## ICT Department

The **Information Communication Technology (ICT) Department** is the central hub of the university's IT infrastructure, operating under the Division of Management Services. It provides seamless interconnectivity between the university community and the outside world.

### Services Provided
- Central **IT Service Desk** for student and staff support
- Campus-wide network and internet management
- Cybersecurity and IT safety management
- IT service delivery for teaching, research, and administration
- Student and staff portal management
- Support for university-wide digital transformation initiatives

### Contact
- ICT Department: ict@babcock.edu.ng
- IT Service Desk: Available on campus at the ICT building

## Computer Laboratories

### University Main Computer Labs

There are **4 medium-size** and **1 large** computer laboratories available for smart teaching, learning, and hands-on computing experience:

| Lab Type | Quantity | Workstations per Lab | Features |
|----------|----------|--------------------|----------|
| Medium-size labs | 4 | 50 networked workstations each | Projector, essential software installed |
| Large lab | 1 | 220 computing stations | 8 servers, smartboard, 2 projectors, internet access |

- All labs have **internet access**
- The large lab doubles as a **smart classroom**

### New Horizon Computer Learning Center

A dedicated computing training centre on campus with:
- **5+ computer training rooms** with a total of ** over 400 desktop computers**
- **5+ projectors** across training rooms
- **several programme examination room** with 8 workstations for certification exam preparation
- Provides computing fundamentals and application hands-on training
- Offers computing **certification preparation services**
- The new NH building was commissioned in 2025 by ex-VC **Professor Ademola S. Tayo**, serving as the hub for Computing students across the entire school.

## Student & Staff Portal

Students access the following services via the **Babcock University Management and Information System (UMIS)**:
- Course registration
- Fee payment and balance checking
- Results and transcript requests
- Hall/accommodation registration
- Academic calendar and timetable
- Exam scheduling
