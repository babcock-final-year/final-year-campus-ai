**Sciences & Health:**
Anatomy, Biol (Biology), BIO-CHEM (Biochemistry), Chem (Chemistry), MICRO-BIO (Microbiology), NUTRITION, MED-LAB (Medical Laboratory Science), NURSING, Nursing Group A/B/C, Nursing Direct Entry, PUBHEALTH (Public Health), Physiology

**Engineering & Technology:**
Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Soft Eng Group A/B/C/D/E (Software Engineering), DE Software, CS (Computer Science), CS GROUP A/B/C/D/E/F, CIS, CT, IT, IT Group A/B, Cyber Security, Info Systems, Physics/Electronics

**Business & Social Sciences:**
