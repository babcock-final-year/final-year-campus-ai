## General Education (GEDS) Courses

GEDS courses are a defining feature of the Babcock curriculum, reflecting the university's commitment to whole-person education. All undergraduate students are required to take and pass GEDS courses as part of their degree.

**Minimum Pass Grade for GEDS:** 40% (Grade E or above)

---

## Programme Credit Structure

Each undergraduate programme has a **minimum total credit requirement** determined by the department and approved by the Senate. Students must complete all credits in:
- General Education (GEDS) requirements
- Core/Required Courses for the department
- Elective courses within approved limits

A student carries a **minimum of 16 credit units per semester** for full-time status (except final semester and probation).

## Research Project Requirement

All graduating **undergraduate** students are required to complete an approved **research project** prior to graduation. The project is supervised by a departmental lecturer and assessed as part of the final year curriculum.

## Academic Trip Policy (Summary)

- Academic trips must be **captured in the course outline** at the start of the semester.
- Trips require approval from the SVP via the HOD and Dean, then the AVP for Institutional Effectiveness.
- Students on probation are **disqualified** from academic trips.
- All off-campus activities require written submission at least **2 weeks** in advance.
- Participating students must submit a pre-trip assignment and make a class presentation on return.
