# Babcock University — Academic Calendar Structure

**Category:** Academics
**Subcategory:** Academic Calendar
**Source:** Undergraduate Bulletin 2019–2023; Final Year Data Document
**Last Updated:** 7th March 2026
**Format:** Markdown

---

## Overview

Babcock University runs a **two-semester academic year**. The calendar is fixed annually by the university administration and communicated to students before resumption.


---

## General Academic Year Structure

| Period | Approximate Timeframe |
|--------|-----------------------|
| First Semester begins | First Monday of September |
| First Semester ends | Mid-December (exams inclusive) |
| Second Semester begins | Mid-January |
| Second Semester ends | Last weekend of April |
| SIWES Period (eligible programmes) | January – June |
| Post-SIWES Second Semester | First week of July – Last week of August |

---

## Key Events Within Each Semester

| Event | Approximate Timing |
|-------|--------------------|
| Registration / Late Registration | First 1–2 weeks of semester |
| Deadline to Add/Drop Courses | Before Mid-Semester exam date |
| Short Periodic Tests (max. 4) | During semester; not in last 3 weeks of classes |
| Assignment Submission Deadline | 8 calendar days before end of classes |
| Mid-Semester Examinations | Approximately Week 7–8 |
| End of Classes | Week 12–13 |
| Final Semester Examinations | After end of classes |
| Results Released | Within 2 weeks of conclusion of exams (via DCE/SBE review) |
| New Semester Resumption | As per calendar |

---

## Important Academic Deadlines

| Action | Deadline |
|--------|----------|
| Adding a course | Before officially approved Mid-Semester exam date |
| Dropping a course | Before officially approved Mid-Semester exam date |
| Late registration | Published in academic calendar each semester |
| Incomplete grade resolution | End of the following semester (or grade becomes F) |
| Voluntary re-admission application | Within 2 years of voluntary withdrawal |

---

## Departmental Weeks

Each academic division conducts a **Departmental Week** once per academic year to promote inter-departmental engagement, visiting lectures, and student activities. These are scheduled by individual schools and approved by the Office of Institutional Effectiveness.

- All departmental and non-academic week activities: **Sunday – Thursday**
- All social programmes conclude by **8:45 pm**
- Programme arrangements must be submitted for approval at least **two weeks** before the event

---

## Key Notes for Students

- Students are responsible for tracking all academic calendar deadlines.
- Registration is complete only when all required procedures are satisfied.
- The **academic bulletin in force at the time of initial registration** is the binding document between the student and the university.
- Students with unique scheduling issues (e.g., SIWES conflicts) should contact their department and the Registry.
