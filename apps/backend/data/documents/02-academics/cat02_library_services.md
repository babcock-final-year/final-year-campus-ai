## Overview

The **Laz Otti Memorial Library** (formerly the Adekunle Alalade Library) is the main academic library of Babcock University. It is located at the centre of the core academic area on the main campus and serves as the university's primary research and learning resource.

## Collection

- Approximately **73,000 volumes** across all disciplines
- Over **5,000 electronic journals** subscribed
- Over **630 hardcopy journals**
- Bound periodicals, theses, and dissertations
- Newspaper and magazine subscriptions

### Electronic Databases
EBSCOHOST · AGORA · HINARI · LEXIS NEXIS · HEIN ONLINE · MEDLINE COMPLETE · JSTOR · and others

### E-Library Facility
- **135 computer systems** + **30 tablets**
- Accessible via **Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC)**
- Internet access is **free** for all registered students and staff within the library
- Off-campus access to subscribed e-journals available via password issued by the Systems Librarian

---

## Library Regulations

- Valid Babcock University **ID card** required at all times in the library
- No eating inside the library
- Bags must be deposited on shelves at the entrance; remove all valuables before depositing
- **All books are checked** by Security/Library Assistant upon exit
- Furniture must not be moved; do not sit on tables or chair arms
- Discussion and noise are **prohibited** inside the library
- Walkman, radios, and audio devices are not permitted (media materials accessible via earpiece at the Media Centre)

---

## Borrowing / Circulation Rules

| Material Type | Loan Period |
|--------------|-------------|
| Regular books | 2 weeks |
| Reserved materials | 2 hours at a time (renewable) |
| Periodical back issues | Overnight (special arrangement) |
| Newspapers | Special permission only |

- Maximum of **2 books** borrowed at a time
- Renewals permitted only on or before the due date, and only if no hold is placed on the book
- Books returned after closing time are recorded as returned the **following day**
- Taking library books home during holidays is **prohibited** unless by special permission

---

## Library Fines

| Offence | Fine |
|---------|------|
| Overdue 2-week materials | ₦50.00 per day |
| Overdue reserved books | ₦50.00 per hour or part thereof |
| Lost book | Double the replacement cost + ₦1,000 penalty if charged to Bursary |
| Damaged book | Full cost + 15% processing fee + ₦500 penalty if charged to Bursary |

- Students with outstanding fines cannot borrow until fines are settled.

## Computer Laboratories

Four medium computer laboratories and one large laboratory are available for academic use:
- Each medium lab: **50 networked workstations** with essential software and a projector
- Large lab: **220 computing stations** with 8 servers; also a smart classroom with smartboard and 2 projectors
- Internet access available in all laboratories

### New Horizons Computer Learning Centre
- **5 training rooms** with a total of **410 desktop computers** and 5 projectors
- 1 programme examination room with 8 workstations
- Provides computing fundamentals training, application training, and computing certification preparation

---

## Ellen G. White & SDA Research Center

Located within the main library; serves Adventist scholars and researchers in **22 countries** under the West-Central African Division. Houses Spirit of Prophecy materials, early Adventist periodicals, microfilm, and manuscripts. Established in 1990.
