## Payment Rules Summary

- Full fees payable **at registration** — no partial registration
- Sponsored students must arrange payment **before** registration
- Payments via e-transact: UBA, WEMA Bank, Zenith Bank, First Bank
- Balance viewable on **student portal**; detailed statements from Bursary
- No refund for disciplinary dismissal or abscondment; refunds prorated only within first 4 weeks for voluntary withdrawal
