## Admission Routes

Admission into Babcock University undergraduate programmes is **only** through:
1. **UTME** — Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (via JAMB)
2. **Direct Entry (DE)** — Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board Direct Entry

> There is **no other route** for regular undergraduate admission. Only UTME and DE candidates who selected Babcock as **first choice** are eligible for Post-UTME screening.

---

## General Entry Requirements (All Programmes)

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Minimum O'Level qualification** | 5 credits at SSCE/NECO/GCE O'Level or equivalent, in **not more than 2 sittings** (Medicine & Surgery: **one sitting only**) |
| **Accepted O'Level bodies** | WAEC, NECO, NABTEB, GCE, and equivalent recognised boards |
| **English Language** | Credit pass (C6 or above) required in **all programmes** |
| **Mathematics** | Credit pass required for Sciences, Social Sciences, Business/Management, and Law programmes |
| **UTME Score** | Minimum aggregate score of **170** (2025/2026 session) |
| **Post-UTME Screening** | All candidates with acceptable UTME scores must sit the university's Post-UTME screening |

## Provisional Admission

- All offers are **provisional** until credentials are verified by the Registrar.
- Candidates awaiting SSCE/NECO/GCE results may apply but must provide **authenticated results before Matriculation**.
- If results do not meet requirements, admission is **automatically withdrawn**.
- A student is not considered a regular undergraduate candidate until original certificates are submitted.

---

## Placement Tests

New students may be required to sit Placement Tests as determined by the University.

## Constituency Candidates

**Seventh-day Adventist candidates** and **children of Babcock faculty/staff** may be offered admission on a **concessionary basis**, provided they meet the minimum academic requirements for their intended programme.

---

## Post-UTME Candidates Who Did Not Choose Babcock as First Choice

Candidates who did not initially select Babcock as first choice **can still apply**, but must:
- Change their institution of first choice to Babcock University at any accredited **JAMB CBT Centre**
- Complete this change before proceeding with the Post-UTME application

---

## Programme-Specific Requirements

Each programme has specific O'Level subject requirements and UTME subject combinations. The key points are:

- **Sciences and Health programmes** typically require credits in Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Agricultural Science, and Mathematics.
- **Social Sciences** typically require Economics, Mathematics, and English.
- **Arts and Humanities** typically require English Language and relevant Arts subjects.
- **Law** requires English Language, Mathematics, and Literature in English (5-year programme).
- **Medicine (MBBS)** is the most stringent: all 5 required subjects (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, English) must be passed at credit level in **one sitting**; minimum UTME aggregate of **170**.

---

## Graduation Application Note

Final-year students must file a **Graduation Application and Agreement Form** available at the Registry at least one semester before graduation. Financial clearance from the Student Accounts Officer is required before participating in graduation exercises or receiving certificates.
