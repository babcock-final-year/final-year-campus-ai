## Overview

Babcock University offers postgraduate programmes through the **College of Postgraduate Studies (CPGS)**. Postgraduate admission, requirements, and regulations are governed by the separate **Postgraduate Bulletin** and have their own distinct procedures from undergraduate admission.

## General Postgraduate Entry Requirements

> The following are **general guidelines**. Specific requirements vary by programme. Consult the Postgraduate Bulletin or the CPGS directly for exact entry requirements.

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **First Degree** | A recognised Bachelor's degree (minimum Second Class Lower, 2.50 CGPA on 5-point scale, for most programmes) |
| **NYSC** | Evidence of National Youth Service Corps participation or exemption typically required for Nigerian citizens |
| **O'Level Credits** | 5 credits at SSCE/equivalent (including English Language and Mathematics) |
| **Application** | Complete postgraduate application form, transcripts, references, and other required documents |
| **Postgraduate Transfer** | Policies on transfer for postgraduate students are contained in the Postgraduate Bulletin |

---

## Advancement to Candidacy (Final Year)

Postgraduate students in their final year must file an **Advancement to Candidacy Form**, available at the Registry. This must be submitted well before the intended graduation date. Financial clearance is required before participation in graduation or receipt of certificates.
