## Types of Fees

Every student at Babcock University has two categories of expenses:

| Category | Components |
|----------|-----------|
| **Educational** | Tuition, books/course materials, academic fees |
| **Living** | Accommodation (room), meals (board) |

---

## Payment Policy

- Full tuition and general fees are payable **at registration** each semester
- No student will be allowed to register without meeting **full payment requirements**
- Sponsored students must make acceptable arrangements for financial obligations **before registration**
- Students may check their account balances online via the **student portal**
- Detailed statements of account are available from the **Bursary Department** on request

---

## Tuition Guarantee Plan

Students may pay the **full estimated charges** (tuition, room, board, books, and fees) for the entire undergraduate programme **at first semester registration of Year 1**. This "locks in" the tuition rate for the entire programme regardless of subsequent fee increases over the normal 4-year duration.

---

## Payment Methods

Payments can be made via:
- **Online (e-transact)** through authorised bank branches: UBA, WEMA Bank, Zenith Bank, First Bank, or other duly authorised banks using certified tellers or Babcock University Bursary/debit cards
- **Official University receipts** are issued by the cashier for all certified payments

---

## Textbook Costs

- Textbook and supply costs are **not included** in the university fee estimates
- Students are advised to budget **₦30,000–₦40,000 per semester**, depending on the discipline
- Books are available at the **Babcock University Bookshop** (open Monday–Thursday 8:00am–7:00pm; Friday and Sunday 8:00am–3:00pm)


---

## Refund Policy

| Condition | Refund |
|-----------|--------|
| Withdrawal before end of **Week 4** | Prorated based on actual days school was in session |
| Withdrawal after Week 4 | No tuition refund |
| Illness/accident/emergency | May be prorated over the entire semester on a case-by-case basis |
| Disciplinary dismissal or abscondment | **No refund** |

Board and room refunds are prorated based on actual days up to proper check-out and key return.

---

## Course Audit Fees

The tuition charge for **auditing a class** is the **same as regular enrolment tuition**.

---

## Regrading Fee

Applications to have an examination script regraded: **₦5,000.00** (subject to review by university administration).

---

## Family Discount

A **5% discount** on net tuition and residential fees applies when two or more dependent children from the same family are concurrently enrolled full-time at Babcock. See `cat03_scholarships_financial_aid.md` for full eligibility criteria.

---

## Banking on Campus

On-campus banking services are provided by:
- **WEMA Bank PLC**
- **First Bank Nigeria PLC**
- **Zenith Bank PLC**
- **Access Bank PLC**
- **GTBank PLC**
- **Union Bank PLC**
- **UBA Bank PLC**

Students are advised to open a personal bank account to minimise risks of carrying cash and facilitate transfers.

Cash withdrawals from a student's university account are only permitted if there is an excess from labour credits or other non-tagged sources. Sponsored students require their sponsor's express permission for any withdrawals.
