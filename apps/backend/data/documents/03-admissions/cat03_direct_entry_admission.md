## What is Direct Entry?

Direct Entry (DE) is an admission route that allows candidates with **post-secondary qualifications** to enter undergraduate programmes at **200 Level** (Year 2), bypassing 100 Level. It is managed by JAMB alongside regular UTME admission.

DE candidates typically complete their degree in **3 years** (6 semesters) instead of 4 years.

---

## General DE Requirements

All Direct Entry candidates must:
1. Meet the **same O'Level requirements** as UTME candidates for their chosen programme
2. Additionally hold **one or more** of the following post-secondary qualifications:
   - **GCE Advanced Level** — minimum 2 passes in relevant subjects
   - **IJMB** (Interim Joint Matriculation Board) — minimum 2 passes
   - **JUPEB** (Joint Universities Preliminary Examinations Board) — minimum acceptable score
   - **National Diploma (ND)** — minimum Upper Credit in a relevant discipline
   - **Higher National Diploma (HND)** — minimum Upper Credit in a relevant discipline
   - **NCE** (Nigeria Certificate in Education) — minimum credit level passes in relevant subjects
   - **First Degree** (B.Sc./B.A. or equivalent) — minimum Third Class

---

## GEDS Requirement for DE Students

Direct Entry students are **not exempt** from General Education (GEDS) courses. They must take all GEDS courses from the point of entry. While credit may be granted for specific courses passed in recognised diploma programmes, **no DE candidate shall be granted whole session exemptions** by university policy.

---

## DE Entry Levels by Qualification

| Qualification | Entry Level |
|--------------|------------|
| A'Level / IJMB / JUPEB passes | 200 Level |
| OND / ND (Upper Credit) | 200 Level |
| HND (Upper Credit) | 200 Level (some programmes: 300 Level) |
| NCE Credit passes | 200 Level |
| First Degree (3rd Class or above) | 200 Level |

> **Engineering programmes** (B.Eng.) — HND holders with good results may enter at 200 Level for the 4-year DE track.
> **Agriculture (B.Agric.)** — HND holders are eligible for 300 Level entry for the 5-year programme.

## DE Requirements by Programme Group

### Arts & Humanities (History, English, French, Religious Studies)
- 5 O'Level credits including English + relevant subject
- **Plus:** 2 A'Level passes in relevant Arts subjects
- OR: NCE / ND at credit level in a relevant area

### Education
- 5 O'Level credits in required subjects
- **Plus:** 2 A'Level passes; OR merit in relevant Diploma; OR 2 NCE passes; OR IJMB/Cambridge/IB from recognised institution

### Music
- 5 O'Level credits including English
- 2 of which at A'Level
- OR ND/NCE credit in Music + 3 other SSCE credits

### Management Sciences (Accounting, Finance, Business Admin, Marketing)
- 5 O'Level credits including English, Maths, and relevant commercial subjects
- **Plus:** 2 A'Level papers (one must be Accounting/Economics/Commerce for Accounting; Mathematics for Business/Marketing)
- OR: ND Upper Credit in relevant discipline
- OR: HND Upper Credit (HND Accounting applicants may enter a **2-year programme**)

### Information Resources Management
- 5 O'Level credits
- **Plus:** 2 A'Level passes; OR OND Upper Credit; OR HND Upper Credit; OR first degree (3rd class+); OR acceptable JUPEB score

### Social Sciences (Economics, Political Science, Mass Comm, Social Work, Public Admin)
- 5 O'Level credits including English, Maths, and relevant subjects
- **Plus:** 2 A'Level passes in Social Science subjects (Economics compulsory for Economics DE)
- OR Diploma at merit level in relevant discipline (Mass Communication)

### Sciences (Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Microbiology, Biochemistry)
- 5 O'Level credits including English, Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Biology
- **Plus:** 2 A'Level passes in relevant Science subjects (GCE/IJMB/JUPEB)

### Computing & Engineering
- 5 O'Level credits including English, Maths, Physics, Chemistry
- **Plus:** 2 A'Level passes in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry (GCE/IJMB/JUPEB)
- OR: ND in relevant Engineering Technology (good result) for 200 Level

### Health Sciences (Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Public Health, MLS, Nutrition)
- 5 O'Level credits including English, Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Biology
- **Plus:** A'Level credits in Physics/Chemistry/Biology/Zoology
- OR: First Degree in relevant Biological/Physical Sciences

### Nursing Science
- 5 O'Level credits: English, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics
- A'Level in relevant subjects (min. B grade)
- OR: Registered Nurses (RN) / Registered Midwives (RM) with SSCE credits admitted at 200 Level

### Environmental Sciences (Architecture, Estate Management)
- 5 O'Level credits in required subjects
- GCE A'Level/HSC/IJMB in 2 relevant subjects for 200 Level
- OR: ND in Architecture/Estate Management/Surveying/Town Planning

> **Note:** Medicine (MBBS) does not have a Direct Entry option.
