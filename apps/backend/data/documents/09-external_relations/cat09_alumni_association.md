## Overview

Babcock University has produced over **21,000 alumni** spanning graduates from its three institutional eras: ACWA (1962–1975), ASWA (1975–1999), and Babcock University (2003–present). Alumni are spread across Nigeria and internationally, working in medicine, law, politics, entertainment, academia, business, and public service.

The first set of university-level graduates under the Babcock University name was released in **2003** — nearly 500 graduates from multiple disciplines.

### Mission

To foster lifelong connections among alumni, support the university's vision, and contribute to the growth and impact of the BU global community — through networking, mentorship, career development, and community outreach.

### Services & Programmes

- **Job Board** *(coming soon)* — curated career opportunities for alumni
- **Mentorship Programme** — connecting recent graduates with established alumni
- **Alumni Connectivity Day** — "A Day with the Stars" held annually during Graduation Week (Amphitheater)
- **Parent Support** — parent conferences, referrals, and consultancy
- **Alumni Homecoming** — Law School holds annual homecoming every October
