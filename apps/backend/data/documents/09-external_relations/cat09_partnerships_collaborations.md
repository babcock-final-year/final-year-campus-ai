## Overview

Babcock University maintains a growing portfolio of international academic partnerships, MOUs, and industry collaborations. These relationships support student exchange, joint research, professional development, and community outreach. BU's international engagement is coordinated by the **Office of Research, Innovation and International Cooperation (RIIC)**, led by **Prof. Grace O. Tayo**.

> **Note:** Partnership statuses change. Verify each entry with the RIIC office or BU website before relying on them as active.

## GRASP International Award

Babcock University has received recognition under the **Grants for Research and School Partnerships (GRASP) International Award** — a competitive programme supporting academic partnerships between institutions in different countries.
