## Overview

The Babcock University cafeteria is a central campus facility serving all resident students. It has a capacity of approximately **5,000 persons**. Cafeteria service is mandatory for resident students — meals are included in the fees.

### Meal Times

| **Lunch** | 12:00 pm – 3:00 pm |
| **Dinner** | 5:00 pm – 8:00 pm |

## Cafeteria Regulations

- Only cafeteria food is permitted inside the cafeteria. **Bringing outside food** into the cafeteria: **30 demerit points**
- Taking cutlery or utensils from the cafeteria without permission: **30 demerit points**
- Students should not chew or eat in classrooms or the library: **40 demerit points**
- Selling or transferring meal tickets without security clearance: **60 demerit points**
- Appropriate dress code applies in the cafeteria at all times, including during Sabbath hours
