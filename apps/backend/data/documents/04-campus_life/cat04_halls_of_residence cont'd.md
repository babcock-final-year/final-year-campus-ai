## Hall Check-In / Check-Out Rules

- Check-in via the **Hall Administrator** using the official Check-In Form
- Checking in without hall administration knowledge: **₦1,000/day + immediate eviction**
- Failing to check in after online registration: **₦1,000/day fine**
- Check-out requires the official **Check-Out Form** from the Hall Administrator
- Improper check-out with key: **₦2,000 + lock/door replacement cost**
- Off-campus students using hall facilities: **₦1,000/day** (regular) · **₦2,000/day** (premium/classic)
