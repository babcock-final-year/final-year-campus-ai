# Babcock University — Dress Code Policy

**Category:** Campus Life
**Subcategory:** Dress Code
**Source:** Final Year Data Document; BU Student Handbook 2021–2024
**Last Updated:** 7th March, 2026
**Format:** Markdown

---

## Overview

The Babcock University dress code aims to present a **corporate outlook** at all times, consistent with the university's Christian and Adventist values. All students — resident and non-resident — are required to comply at all times on campus and at any off-campus university activity.

> Dress code violations carry **20 demerit points** and may result in being denied entry to class.

---

## Female Students

### Permitted
- Skirts and blouses, sweaters and skirts, or other formal combinations
- African or Western dress mode for Sabbath/formal occasions (not a mix of both)
- Proper fitting slacks for outdoor recreational activities (AYM, hiking, community service, sports) — must still conform to dress regulations
- Moderate artificial hair (braided or attached) — must not exceed shoulder length; must match natural hair colour

### Required Specifications
- Skirts/skirt suits must cover the knees by **at least 3 inches below** at all times — whether standing, sitting, bending, walking, or kneeling
- Skirts must not have slits up to the knee
- No bare midriff, sheer or tight blouses that expose undergarments or intimate body parts (back, chest, thigh, abdomen, armpits, shoulders) at any position
- Shawls, sheers, or scarves **cannot** be used to cover sleeveless or low neckline tops
- Only **cotton and similar non-stretchy materials** are allowed for skirts and dresses (no Lycra, bodycon, "condom", or "water" materials)

### Prohibited
- Spaghetti strap blouses
- Tight-fitted elastic/stretchy wears
- Show-back dresses
- Low neckline gowns or blouses
- Above-the-knee dresses/skirts
- Net or lace wear that exposes the body
- Pencil trousers / jump-up trousers
- Facial makeup, lip lining, coloured lip gloss, coloured nail polish, artificial nails, eye shadow, fake eyelashes, heavy makeup, bleaching
- Contact lenses not medically recommended
- Unnatural hair colour / dyed hair
- Hair that covers the ear
- Artificial hair below shoulder length or of unnatural colour

---

## Male Students

### Permitted
- Trousers, dress shirts, suits, African attire
- Decent shirts (short or long sleeve) tucked in with trousers, with or without tie
- Complete English, French, or African suits
- Complete African attire (cap must be **removed** during church services)
- Shoes and buckled dress sandals; leather slippers with African attire **only**

### Required Specifications
- Trousers must be **ankle length** (not under-length or over-length)
- Shirts must be **tucked in** at all times
- Long sleeves must **not** be rolled up

### Prohibited
- Baggy, boot-cut, saggy, or pencil trousers
- Sagging of any kind at any time
- Bushy hair or beards
- Unnatural hair colour, bizarre hairstyles, braiding
- Bare feet, bare-bodied, or bathroom slippers outside the hall or at reception

---

## General Rules (All Students)

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Jeans** | Prohibited in **all forms** — shirts, jackets, shorts, skirts, trousers, coloured, bleached, etc. Not permitted at any Babcock University activity on or off campus |
| **T-shirts / Polo shirts** | Only permitted on **approved club days** and **Sundays only**; must be decent without objectionable inscriptions |
| **Shorts** | Prohibited in lectures, worship, library, cafeteria, or any formal gathering; permitted only at the **sports arena** |
| **Jewellery** | Prohibited — bracelets, earrings, necklaces, etc. Wedding rings are optional. Defaulters have jewellery seized |
| **Face caps** | Unacceptable |
| **Noisy/pointed heels** | Not permitted (damage floors) |
| **Religious/radical connotation dresses** | Not allowed |
| **Bizarre hairstyles** | Not allowed for any gender |

### Slippers
- Bathroom slippers are **not permitted** outside the hall of residence or at the reception
- May only be worn within the hall of residence room/corridor

### Sabbath Dress
- During Sabbath services, attire should be modest and formal — either African or Western mode (not combined)
- Similar standards apply in the cafeteria during Sabbath hours

### Sports Attire
- Shorts for sports must be worn **only** at the sports arena
- Sports attire must comply with overall dress regulations for female students

### Graduating Students
- Specific dress regulations apply for graduating students leading up to and during graduation exercises. Consult the Registry and VP for Student Development for current guidelines.

---

## Enforcement

- **Teaching staff** will send out students who arrive to class badly dressed
- **20 demerit points** for dress code violations, with additional sanctions possible for repeat offences
- Jewellery is seized at point of detection
